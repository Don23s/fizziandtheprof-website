Meet the Characters

Get to know Fizzi, Pico and Professor Pino – the team behind every adventure.

Main Character

Fizzi

Young Fox · Curious Learner

Fizzi is a young, curious fox who discovers the world of numbers and learning step by step. Fizzi is never afraid to make mistakes – in fact, those are often the best moments to learn something new. With boundless energy and an open heart, Fizzi invites every child to join the adventure.

Curious Playful Brave Friendly
Best Friend

Pico

Little Songbird · Loyal Companion

Pico is Fizzi's loyal best friend – a small, lively songbird who is always by Fizzi's side. Pico asks the questions that everyone is thinking, cheers Fizzi on when things get tough, and keeps the mood light with a well-timed joke. Together, they make the perfect learning duo.

Loyal Witty Caring Cheerful
The Teacher

Professor Pino

Older Fox · Wise Mentor

Professor Pino is a wise, gentle older fox who guides Fizzi and Pico through the world of knowledge. Patient and quietly humorous, he has a gift for asking just the right question at the right moment. He never simply gives the answer – he loves to watch his students think their way to the solution themselves.

Wise Patient Humorous Inspiring

Learn. Laugh. Grow.
